# Evidence Glossary

## Client
The person or organization Cece Law represents. A client may have multiple matters.
Top-level owner of all legal work. Contains name and contact information.

## Matter
A discrete legal case or engagement. Primary organizational container for all evidence,
transcripts, annotations, issues, and exports. Belongs to a Client.
`allow_remote_ai` defaults to `false` on every matter.

## EvidenceItem
A single ingested file — video, audio, document, image, or other.
The atomic unit of evidence. Has a SHA-256 hash computed at ingest that never changes.
Status progresses: ingested → processing → transcribed → reviewed → archived.

## MediaAsset
A physical file on disk associated with an EvidenceItem — either the original file
or a derived copy (extracted audio, clip, redacted version). Originals are never mutated.
Derived assets link back to their source via `parent_asset_id`.

## Transcript
The complete text output generated by transcribing an audio or video EvidenceItem.
Immutable after generation. User corrections are expressed as Annotations, not edits to the transcript.
Records the ASR model name, version, and whether transcription was local or remote.

## TranscriptSegment
A single timestamped utterance within a Transcript.
Has `start_ms`, `end_ms`, `text`, `confidence`, and an optional `speaker_label`.
Annotations and Excerpts attach to specific segments.

## Annotation
A user-created note or observation attached to an EvidenceItem or TranscriptSegment.
May reference an Issue and carry Tags. Annotaton source is permanently recorded as
`HUMAN` or `AI` and cannot be changed. Soft-deleted only — never hard-deleted.

## Tag
A lightweight label applied to Annotations for categorization.
May be global (available across all matters) or matter-scoped.

## Issue
A named legal question or theme grouping related Annotations and Excerpts.
Examples: "Timeline of Events", "Witness Credibility", "Chain of Possession".
AI-created issues require explicit human acceptance before they are saved.

## Excerpt
A user-defined, bounded selection of content — a time range in a video/audio file,
or a character span in a transcript — prepared for citation or export.
Immutable after creation; changes require a new Excerpt record.

## ReviewStatus
Tracks the human review state of an EvidenceItem.
Values: `unreviewed` → `in_progress` → `reviewed`; any state → `flagged`.
Every transition is recorded in the chain of custody.

## ChainOfCustodyEvent
An immutable, append-only audit record of every significant action on an EvidenceItem.
Actions include: INGESTED, AUDIO_EXTRACTED, TRANSCRIBED, ANNOTATION_CREATED,
CLIP_GENERATED, EXPORTED, and more. Cannot be updated or deleted.

## ExportBundle
A compiled, court-ready output package assembled from selected evidence, excerpts,
and annotations. Contains a signed manifest of all included file hashes.
Immutable after creation. Every export writes a ChainOfCustodyEvent with the manifest hash.
