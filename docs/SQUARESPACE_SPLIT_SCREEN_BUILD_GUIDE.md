4. Practice Area 2
5. About
6. Contact
7. Booking / Schedule Consultation
8. Payments
9. Privacy Policy
10. Disclaimer

## Split-Screen Workflow
- Left panel: `master/CECE_LAW_WEBSITE_COPY_v1.0.md`
- Right panel: Squarespace 7.1 Fluid Engine editor
- Locate target page by `COPY_REF`.
- Copy only the section needed; avoid rewriting whole page in Squarespace.
- Apply SEO title/meta in page SEO settings before final publish.

## Squarespace SEO Field Placement
- Page Settings → SEO
  - SEO Title: paste from block `SEO Title`
  - SEO Description: paste from block `Meta Description`

## Paste Discipline QA Checklist
- H1 is unique for each page.
- Disclaimers are present and unedited.
- No legal-advice language appears.
- No promise/guarantee language appears.
- CTA flow is consistent: call → book → intake → payments.
- PracticePanther appears only where operationally relevant as system of record.
- SEO title and meta are filled and within guidance.
- Local language is natural and does not claim unverified office presence.
